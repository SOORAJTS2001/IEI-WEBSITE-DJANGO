ABOUT US

Institution of Engineers (India) is the national organization of engineers in India. We are the world's biggest multidisciplinary professional society for engineering and technology. To encourage the progress of engineering and its applications at a student level, we organise numerous seminars, hands-on workshops, and interview preparations depending on student demands. We also provide competence certifications, scholarships, honours, and benefactions to people and organisations to improve, develop, and recognise their technical knowledge and practise.
